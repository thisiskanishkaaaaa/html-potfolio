The Best Web Series According To Kanishka

My Top 3 Web Series Of All Time.

Friends

A group of six young adults who are either roommates or neighbours in New York City's Greenwich Village. Much of the show takes place in the friends' apartments as they visit one another.

Gilmore girls

This show follows the lives of a 30-something single mother Lorelai Gilmore and her intellectual teenage daughter Rory Gilmore.

Gossip Girls

Privileged teens living on the Upper East Side of New York can hide no secret from the ruthless blogger who is always watching.
